    Churn Analysis

    This Churn Analysis Dashboard offers a comprehensive overview of customer retention and churn patterns within a telecommunications service. It presents high-level metrics such as a total customer base of 6,418, a churn rate of 27%, and a breakdown of 1,732 total churned customers versus 411 new joiners.     Image-or-Video-to-Text-Extractor-Generator

    An image or video to text extractor is a tool or technology that automatically analyzes visual content—such as pictures or video frames—and extracts readable text from it. This process typically involves Optical Character Recognition (OCR) for images and frame-by-frame text detection in videos. These tools are widely used in scenarios like digitizing printed documents, extracting subtitles from videos, recognizing license plates, or converting handwritten notes into digital format. In the case of videos, key frames are analyzed either continuously or at set intervals to capture and transcribe any visible text. Modern AI-powered extractors can even handle complex layouts, stylized fonts, and low-resolution inputs, making them incredibly useful in various fields like media archiving, education, accessibility enhancement, and surveillance.

    Adventure Analysis

    The Adventure Works Sales Analysis dashboard offers a comprehensive time-based view of the company’s sales performance, highlighting key business metrics and behavioral insights. With a total quantity sold of 550 units, the business generated $69.48 million in revenue and $28.18 million in profit, reflecting a strong 41% profit margin and consistent year-over-year growth. The data reveals 2006 as a peak performance year, accounting for 67.1% of total profit (approximately $31.57M), with March, May, and June emerging as the most profitable months. Additionally, the analysis showcases a significant behavioral trend where weekdays contributed 71.9% of total profit, with Friday and Monday leading as the top revenue days. Quarterly breakdowns reveal Q2 as the highest-earning period with $8.65 million (31%), followed by Q1 at $6.10 million (28%). The dashboard's interactive filters—featuring dynamic slicers for year, month, and region (including countries like the US, UK, Canada, and Australia)—empower users to explore performance trends and regional sales insights with ease. Overall, the dashboard provides actionable insights that help stakeholders understand seasonal dynamics, customer behavior, and strategic opportunities for growth.
